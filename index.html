Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [Date] | Effective Date: [Date]

Welcome to Bank on It ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Bank on It ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the data practices described in this Privacy Policy, you should not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our App.

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect email address, password (encrypted and securely stored), first name and last name, charitable organization preference, and profile information.
  • Goal and Task Data: When you use the App, we collect goal titles and descriptions, task lists and completion status, deadlines and time parameters, partner invitations and collaboration data, and progress tracking information.
  • Payment Information: When you make donations through the App, we collect payment card information (processed securely through Stripe), billing details (including country), transaction history, and donation amounts and recipient organizations.

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device model and operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in the App, goal creation and completion rates, and task completion patterns.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your Google account email, display name, and Google account profile picture (if available).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide and Maintain Our Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable goal and task creation and management
  • Facilitate partner collaboration features
  • Process payments and donations
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service-related communications

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve App functionality
  • Develop new features and services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Conduct research and analytics

2.3 To Generate AI-Powered Task Recommendations

  • Send goal information to OpenAI to generate personalized task lists
  • Improve task generation algorithms based on user feedback

2.4 For Communication

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send you important updates about the App
  • Notify you about changes to our services or policies

2.5 For Advertising

  • Display advertisements through Google Mobile Ads
  • Measure and analyze advertising effectiveness
  • Personalize ads based on your interests (where applicable)

We integrate with several third-party services that help us operate the App. These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

3.1 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for user authentication, Cloud Firestore database for storing your goals, tasks, and profile data, and analytics and crash reporting.

Data Stored: User profiles, goals, tasks, authentication credentials

Location: Google Cloud Platform

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In to allow you to authenticate using your Google account.

Data Collected: Email, display name, profile information

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Stripe

We use Stripe for secure payment processing and donation transactions.

Data Collected: Payment card information, billing details, transaction data

Data Stored: We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers

Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

3.4 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API to generate AI-powered task lists based on your goals.

Data Sent: Goal titles, descriptions, and time parameters

Data Stored: OpenAI may retain API data according to their policies

Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

3.5 Google Mobile Ads

We use Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements in the App.

Data Collected: Device identifiers, advertising IDs, usage data, approximate location

Purpose: Ad targeting and analytics

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.6 Partner Sharing

When you invite a partner to collaborate on a goal:

  • We share goal details and task information with that partner
  • Partners can view your progress and task completion status
  • You can remove partners or revoke access at any time

3.7 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights and property, prevent fraud or abuse, or protect the safety of users or others.

3.8 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: For user data, goals, and tasks
  • Firebase Authentication: For authentication credentials
  • Stripe: For payment information (we do not store full payment card details)

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
